Cloaking tools serve different content to search engine crawlers versus human visitors, enabling highly deceptive SEO strategies.

Pricing: $299-999/mo
Features: Bot detection, geo-targeting, A/B testing
üëÅÔ∏è Adspect
Advertising-focused cloaking platform. Bypasses ad platform moderation by showing compliant pages to reviewers, actual affiliate offers to real traffic. JavaScript-based detection.

üé≠ JustCloakit

üìä Keitaro
Advanced tracker and cloaker combined. Traffic distribution, conversion tracking, bot filtering, and cloaking in one platform. Self-hosted solution popular in gray-hat affiliate circles.

‚öôÔ∏è Custom Scripts

CTR MANIPULATION SERVICES

Click-through rate manipulation attempts to improve rankings by generating fake organic clicks from search results.

üñ±Ô∏è SerpClix
Human-powered CTR service. Real users click your listings from search results for payment. Geotargeted clicks, real devices, varied ISPs. Most established CTR manipulation service.
Pricing: $99-500/mo (volume-based)
Features: Real users, geo-targeting, gradual increase
ü¶ë Kraken CTR
Bot-based CTR manipulation using residential proxies and emulated browser behavior. Cheaper than human services but higher detection risk. Offers bulk packages for agencies.
Pricing: $49-247/mo
Features: Automated bots, residential proxies, bulk orders
üë• Microworkers
General microtask platform used for CTR manipulation. Post tasks for workers to search keywords and click your listings. Less sophisticated than dedicated CTR services.
Pricing: Pay per task ($0.10-0.50/click)
Features: Global workers, manual tasks, variable quality
