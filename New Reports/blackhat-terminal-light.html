◈ DEFENSE STRATEGIES

Monitoring: Regular backlink audits, traffic pattern analysis, rank tracking for anomalies, Google Search Console alerts, negative SEO detection tools.

Prevention: Focus on E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authoritativeness, Trustworthiness), quality over quantity, genuine user engagement, legitimate link building, compliance monitoring.
