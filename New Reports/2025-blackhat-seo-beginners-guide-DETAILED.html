How to Show Different Content to Search Engines vs. Users
DIFFICULTY: ADVANCED

What is Cloaking?

Cloaking is a technique that shows completely different content to search engine crawlers (Googlebot) versus real human visitors. This allows you to:

  • Rank high for valuable keywords with SEO-optimized content for bots
  • Show ads, affiliate offers, or malware to actual visitors
  • Hide illegal/unethical content from search engines while monetizing human traffic
  • Manipulate rankings without triggering immediate penalties
‚ö†Ô∏è WHY THIS IS ILLEGAL:
Cloaking violates Google's Webmaster Guidelines, constitutes fraud (showing false information), and in cases involving malware/phishing, violates the Computer Fraud & Abuse Act (CFAA) which carries penalties of up to 20 years in federal prison.

How Cloaking Works - Technical Overview

Core Concept: Your server detects WHO is requesting the page (Googlebot vs human) and dynamically serves different HTML content based on:
  • IP Address: Googlebot uses specific IP ranges (Google publishes these)
  • User-Agent String: Googlebot identifies itself with a specific user-agent
  • Reverse DNS: Googlebot IPs resolve to googlebot.com domains

Prerequisites & Requirements

What You Need:

  • Web Server: Apache or Nginx (with mod_rewrite or similar)
  • Hosting: VPS or dedicated server with root access ($5-50/month)
  • Domain: Any registered domain ($10-15/year)
  • Programming Knowledge: PHP, Apache config, or JavaScript
  • Tools: FTP client (FileZilla), text editor (Sublime/VS Code)

What is a Private Blog Network?

A PBN is a collection of 10-100+ websites you own and control, built specifically to link back to your "money site" (the site you actually want to rank). By creating fake authority through expired domains, you manipulate Google into thinking your money site is popular and trustworthy.

How PBNs Manipulate Rankings:
  • Each PBN site has existing domain authority from previous owner
  • You acquire expired domains that still have quality backlinks
  • Post "content" on PBN sites with contextual links to your money site
  • Google sees these links as natural endorsements
  • Your money site's rankings improve artificially

Complete PBN Setup Guide - Step by Step

1 Find Expired Domains with Authority

Goal: Find domains that expired but still have valuable backlinks and domain authority

Tools Needed:
  • ExpiredDomains.net - Free tool to find recently expired domains
  • DomCop - Paid service ($47/month) with better filtering
  • SpamZilla - Advanced metrics and spam score checking ($197/month)
  • Ahrefs - Check backlink quality ($99-999/month)
Step-by-Step Domain Hunting:
Using ExpiredDomains.net (Free Method):
  1. Go to expireddomains.net
  2. Click "Deleted Domains" tab
  3. Set filters:
    • Archive.org: Yes (must have been a real site)
    • Backlinks: 10+ (shows it had some authority)
    • Domain Age: 5+ years (older = better)
    • TLD: .com, .net, .org only (avoid spammy extensions)
  4. Sort by "ABY" (Archive Birth Year) - oldest first
  5. Click on promising domains to see details
Evaluating Domain Quality: 
GOOD DOMAIN CHECKLIST:
‚òê Domain Authority (DA): 20+ (check with Moz or Ahrefs)
‚òê Referring Domains: 10+ unique sites linking to it
‚òê Archive.org History: Was a real site (not parked or spam)
‚òê Backlink Quality: Links from .edu, .gov, or reputable sites
‚òê Spam Score: Under 30% (Moz)
‚òê Clean History: No adult/gambling/pharma in past
‚òê Brandable Name: Looks legitimate, not keyword-stuffed
‚òê No Manual Penalties: Check Google Search Console if possible

AVOID IF:
‚úó Mostly comment/forum spam links
‚úó All backlinks from same C-class IP
‚úó Previous Penguin/Panda penalty
‚úó Trademark issues (brand names)
‚úó Exact match domain (EMD) with keyword stuffing

  • Different registrars for each domain
  • Use privacy protection/WHOIS guard

Using same Google Analytics code, same AdSense account, same theme, or same hosting across all sites will get your entire PBN burned. Diversify everything.

4 Insert Money Site Links Strategically

This is the actual manipulation part - adding links from your PBN to your money site.

Link Placement Rules:
  • Only 1-2 money site links per PBN site (more looks spammy)
  • Place links in contextual content (middle of article, not footer)
  • Use varied anchor text:
    • 30% exact match ("best credit cards 2025")
    • 30% partial match ("click here for credit cards")
    • 20% branded ("YourBrand credit guide")
    • 20% generic ("read more", "click here")
  • Make links look natural - write sentence around them
Example Link Insertion:
BAD (Obvious PBN link):
"Check out this great website about credit cards: 
<a href="money-site.com">best credit cards 2025</a>"

GOOD (Natural-looking link):
"When I was researching financial planning last month, I found 
<a href="money-site.com/credit-guide">this comprehensive guide</a> 
that really helped me understand different credit card rewards programs."
Linking Schedule:
  1. Build PBN sites with 10-15 articles each (no money site links yet)
  2. Wait 30 days
  3. Add first money site link to 3-4 PBN sites
  4. Wait 2 weeks
  5. Add second round of links to different PBN sites
  6. Continue drip-feeding links over 3-6 months
‚ö†Ô∏è Red Flags That Get PBNs Detected:

  • Only outbound links are to money site (no other external links)
  • All links use exact match anchor text
  • PBN sites have no incoming links (dead sites)
  • Same IP addresses across multiple PBN sites
